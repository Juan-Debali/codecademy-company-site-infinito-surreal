“Infinite” that is projected in the possibilities of drawing, line and imagination.

Calling “surreal” the “subterranean reality”, hidden in ourselves, under the everyday imaginary, or above it, if you like. Something specific captured in that infinity, to stop the immediate mind and deceive it, or free it for a few moments.

About

Born in Uruguay, living in Brazil since 1998, graduated in architecture and urbanism in 2008, from the Federal University of Viçosa in Minas Gerais. He moved to Florianópolis and has been practicing his profession in several offices in the city. Initiated in drawing since he was a teenager, in Uruguay, where he had contact with the first disciplines of technical drawing, he has maintained the hobby of free drawing throughout all these years.
Today the hobby has become a reason for living among others. Gradually, art made him dedicate hours to the table and to the computer, where he has been treating the drawings to give them the final look, with different colors and effects. Since 2018 he has prepared material to exhibit and start selling his works, which range from cartooning to the abstract world, from multi-scenic compositions to compositions created through automatism.
